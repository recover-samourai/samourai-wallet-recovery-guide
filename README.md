# Samourai Wallet Recovery Guide (2026)

**Website:** https://samourai-wallet.io

Free, community-driven recovery resource for former Samourai Wallet users. After the April 24, 2024 DOJ seizure, your Bitcoin is still safe on the blockchain. This project collects step-by-step guides to recover BTC in 30 minutes using Sparrow Wallet or Electrum, including the hidden Whirlpool derivation paths most users never find on their own.

> Your seed phrase controls your Bitcoin. Not the app. Not the founders. Not the DOJ. Recovery is always free.

## Why Samourai Wallet Recovery

The original Samourai Wallet was a privacy-focused Bitcoin wallet featuring Whirlpool CoinJoin, Ricochet routing and PayNym stealth addresses. The mobile app was pulled from Google Play in April 2024, the servers shut down, and founders Keonne Rodriguez and William Hill were sentenced in 2025. None of that affects the Bitcoin itself. The 12-word seed phrase you wrote down on day one is still the only thing that controls your UTXOs.

The [Samourai Wallet Recovery Guide](https://samourai-wallet.io/recover-samourai-wallet-funds) walks through exactly what happens when you plug that seed into a compatible desktop wallet, how to spot Whirlpool premix/postmix/bad-bank UTXOs that normally hide behind non-standard derivation paths, and how to move funds to a fresh seed once you have verified the balance.

## About

This repository is maintained by former Samourai Wallet users and privacy advocates. It is not affiliated with the original Samourai Wallet LLC, its founders or their legal counsel. All instructions are derived from public open-source tooling: Sparrow Wallet, Electrum, and the Bitcoin BIP84 / BIP32 derivation specification.

The Free Samourai Movement holds that running privacy-preserving open-source software is a protected activity. Writing code is speech. Self-custody is not a crime. The [Free Samourai Wallet Movement](https://samourai-wallet.io/free-samourai-wallet-movement) page tracks advocacy updates and public petitions.

## Warnings

- Never enter your seed phrase into a website. The only safe place to type a 12-word phrase is an offline Sparrow or Electrum installation from a verified source.
- Never pay a "recovery service" to recover your wallet. Recovery is free. Anyone demanding payment is running a scam.
- Do not download APK files from random mirrors. The [download hub](https://samourai-wallet.io/samourai-wallet-download-apk) only links to signed Sparrow and Electrum releases.
